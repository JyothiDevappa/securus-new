Physical Guarding

Securus Pte Ltd is a qualified firm equipped with adequate infrastructure and operational personnel to deliver quality services to our clients and the community. Our skilled personnel can manage any situation, and we operate a 24/7 command center to oversee our client locations. We collaborate with government law enforcement agencies to fight both global terrorism and local crime.

Security guard services are crucial in both public and private sectors. The security staff enhance and support the police and other law enforcement bodies in safeguarding lives and property for residential, commercial, industrial, entertainment venues, concerts, trade shows, condominiums, schools, universities, job sites, and various locations. Entrust these unique responsibilities to Securus Pte Ltd. Our experts can assist you in safeguarding your property and individuals. They are highly skilled in managing any type of scenario.

Our management team consists of ex-police officers, public servants, and high-level executives from international corporations. To begin, we will engage our clients in a conversation to better grasp their needs and budget regarding coverage. A survey will take place on site, and corresponding proposals will be suggested according to the client's needs. In situations with budget limitations, where the sole need is restricted security personnel, we will recommend the necessary staff to ensure basic security coverage.
