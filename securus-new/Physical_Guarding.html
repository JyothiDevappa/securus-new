Physical guarding is a component of physical security that involves using security personnel to protect people, property, and assets from harm or loss. It encompasses a range of activities, including access control, surveillance, and response to incidents. Effective physical guarding requires a combination of human expertise and technology to deter, detect, and respond to threats.

SECURUS offers professional and cost-effective Physical security services. We supply and manage security personnel on a 24-hour basis for large MNCs, Embassies, Financial establishments, Educational Institutions, Museums, Hotels, Commercial and Industrial buildings, as well as projects.
