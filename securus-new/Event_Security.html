Our Specialized Event Security Services

Securus Pte Ltd offers a comprehensive array of security services and solutions tailored to our clients and individuals for their particular requirements at concerts, sports events, trade shows, corporate gatherings, expos, and regular meetings. We utilize advanced security programs to protect individuals and property using the most recent security technologies and solutions. Securus Pte Ltd has a command center that operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, serving as a central hub for all Securus event activities. Our team oversees the location from the control center. The Operations Centers provide emergency assistance and support for all client locations to guarantee the safety of our staff and the protection of our clients' assets.

We can send messages from the command center to various locations via the Centralized Automation Monitoring System (CAMS). Securus provides event security management to ensure that the event is conducted in a safe environment, thus allowing you to have peace of mind. Event management encompasses the meticulous planning, organization, and execution of various events, from conferences and trade shows to exhibitions and festivals. It involves a comprehensive process that includes defining event goals, managing budgets, coordinating logistics, selecting venues, and ensuring a positive attendee experience. Effective event management is crucial for creating memorable and successful events that align with the client's vision and objectives. For remote monitoring, we set up CCTV cameras at our locations, configured via IP using CAMS software.

We can subsequently track our clients' whereabouts from any location around the globe. With CAMS, we can save videos, enabling us to access footage whenever needed, particularly in urgent situations. This is the latest technology we utilize to protect our clients' properties with assistance from our technical provider.
