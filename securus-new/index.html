About Our Company

Welcome to Securus

Incorporated in 2009, SECURUS embraces an integrated approach to security, incorporating physical guarding, remote monitoring, event security, in-house training and security consultancy service for private entities. Within 6 years, SECURUS had achieved a grading of S9 in EPPU, this had further advanced our reach to both Government and private entities in Singapore. Focusing on training competency and operational capability, SECURUS ties up with relevant Government agencies to bolster Government efforts to train and better equip security staff. In 2011, SECURUS was granted ATO status and achieved ISO 9000 Status and Biz SAFE Level 4.
