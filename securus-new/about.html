Incorporated in 2009, SECURUS embraces an integrated approach to security, incorporating physical guarding, remote monitoring, event security, in-house training, and security consultancy service for private entities. Within 6 years, SECURUS had achieved a grading of S9 in EPPU, advancing our reach to both Government and private entities in Singapore.

Focusing on training competency and operational capability, SECURUS ties up with relevant Government agencies to bolster Government efforts to train and better equip security staff. In 2011, SECURUS was granted ATO status and achieved ISO 9001 Status and Biz SAFE Level 4.

SECURUS actively works on the ideology that security could be better achieved through technology. Our inauguration aimed to solve the high expectation for increased security in Singapore and we continually search for new technologies to cater to the demand for innovative and advanced solutions. Our commitment has garnered an ‘A’ grading in 2024 & 2025 by the Singapore Police Force, alongside several SSI awards.

It all began with the vision of advocating a mutually rewarding partnership in security. The demand for security is a necessity under stringent requirements for safety-first purposes in various industries. With the mission of securing people and assets through a comprehensive network of technological and human applications, SECURUS cooperates with esteemed organizations like the Monetary Authority of Singapore, Land Transport Authority, Singapore Grand Prix, Housing Development Board, National Institute of Education, Nanyang Academy of Fine Arts, Stamford American School, Penta Ocean Construction Ltd, Mapletree Properties (eastern sector), Orchid Country Club, Singapore Flyer, and Genting Centre (Resorts World Singapore HQ).
